Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Marksheet Management App (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and related services. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information we collect

  • Personal information: Name, email address, school and role (teacher, student, admin).
  • Academic data: Student marks, classes, attendance, and school identifiers.
  • Device information: Device type, OS version, and non-identifying technical data for support.
  • Usage data: App interactions such as login times, feature usage, and error logs.

Note: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless provided by authorized school administrators for educational purposes.

How we use information

  • Service delivery: Provide marksheet management, reporting, and role-based access.
  • Synchronization: Securely sync marks across authorized teachers and devices.
  • Communication: Send notifications related to marks, attendance, and school updates.
  • Security: Detect, prevent, and respond to unauthorized access or misuse.
  • Improvement: Analyze usage to improve features, reliability, and performance.

Legal basis

Our processing is based on legitimate interests in providing educational services, performance of agreements with schools and users, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Where required, we will obtain consent for specific processing activities.

Data protection

  • Encryption: Data is protected in transit via HTTPS and at rest using industry-standard encryption.
  • Access controls: Role-based permissions restrict access to authorized teachers, students, and admins.
  • Secure storage: Data may be stored in reputable cloud services (e.g., Firebase or Supabase) with strong security practices.
  • Minimization: We collect only the data necessary for educational and administrative functions.

Sharing of information

  • Within the school: Shared with authorized administrators and teachers for academic purposes.
  • Service providers: Trusted vendors supporting hosting, analytics, and security under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: Disclosed when required by law, regulation, or to protect safety, rights, and property.
  • No sale of data: We do not sell personal information.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

User rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal and academic data associated with your account.
  • Correction: Request updates to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to school and legal requirements.
  • Objection & restriction: Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities where applicable.

Requests may be routed through your school administrator for verification and compliance.

Children’s privacy

This app is intended for use by schools. Student information is managed by authorized school staff. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without appropriate school or parent involvement, as applicable by local law.

International transfers

Depending on hosting providers, data may be processed in data centers outside your region. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any cross-border transfers consistent with applicable law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated within the app or via contact details provided. Continued use of the app after changes signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
