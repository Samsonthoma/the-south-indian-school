Rules & Regulations

All students must adhere to the following rules and regulations to maintain discipline and academic excellence.

  • Students must arrive at school on time and be punctual for all classes.
  • Wearing the prescribed school uniform is mandatory.
  • Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students is expected at all times.
  • Use of mobile phones during school hours is strictly prohibited.
  • Any form of bullying, harassment, or misconduct will result in disciplinary action.
  • Students must complete and submit all assignments and homework on time.
  • Maintaining cleanliness in classrooms and school premises is essential.
  • Students should participate actively in school events and activities.
  • Leaving the school premises without permission is strictly forbidden.
  • Parents are encouraged to attend parent-teacher meetings regularly.
